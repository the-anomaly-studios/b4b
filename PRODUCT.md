# Band for Band

## Register

product

## Users

Band enthusiasts browse HBCU marching-band performances, discover schools, and support favorites through upvotes. Current members and alumni share performances, represent their school, and track the impact of what they upload.

## Product Purpose

Band for Band is the trusted home for discovering, celebrating, and ranking HBCU marching-band performances. The initial product should make browsing feel immediate, give every school a distinct presence, and make leaderboard movement easy to understand. Success means users can quickly find a performance worth watching and understand how their support contributes to school pride.

## Brand Personality

Electric, proud, and credible. The voice celebrates the energy of the stands and field while treating HBCU band culture with knowledge and respect.

## Anti-references

Avoid sterile SaaS dashboards, repetitive card grids, and generic productivity-app styling. Avoid sportsbook conventions, betting language, casino-like urgency, and interfaces that reduce school pride to odds or wagers.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the performance. Video, school identity, and context come before interface decoration.
2. Make school pride legible. Rankings and votes should feel competitive but transparent, fair, and celebratory.
3. Respect the culture. Use informed, specific language and never flatten distinct band traditions into generic sports content.
4. Keep discovery moving. Browsing, filtering, and moving between a school and its performances should require minimal effort.
5. Earn trust through clarity. Explain ranking logic, distinguish real data from placeholders, and make actions provide immediate feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support full keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, sufficient contrast, descriptive media labels, captions when available, and reduced-motion preferences. Never rely on school colors alone to communicate rank, state, or action.
